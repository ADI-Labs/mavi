WTF is WTFColumbia?

WTFColumbia (What the Fuck Columbia) is a student run web-app dedicated to helping students complain about everything they want to change about Columbia and hopefully enact real change.

WTF is the point?

All the cool kids are doing it! It's obviously fun and interesting to see what your peers hate about this school, but if you need even more incentive, posts that receive enough updates are pushed to ESC and CCSC for actual discussion.
